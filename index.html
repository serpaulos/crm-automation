FAQ

Questions answered

Everything you need to decide with confidence.

Do I need to know how to code?
No. You import the workflow file into n8n, connect your Google account, and add your first lead. The setup guide walks you through every single step with screenshots.
What tools do I need? Are they free?
n8n is free and open-source — self-host it with Docker at no cost, or use n8n.cloud from €20/month. Gmail and Google Sheets are free with any Google account. That's everything you need.
How long does setup take?
Most users are up and running in 30 to 60 minutes on their first setup, including the Google OAuth2 configuration. The guide covers every step.
Can I customise the follow-up emails and pipeline stages?
Yes. Edit the email copy directly inside n8n. Add or rename pipeline stages by updating the Google Sheet and adjusting one IF node — takes under 5 minutes.
Will it send emails from my own address?
Yes. All emails go out from your authenticated Gmail account, so they arrive looking like a normal email from you — not from a third-party service.
What if it doesn't work for me?
If the workflow doesn't run after following the setup guide, contact me directly and I will fix it personally. That's a personal guarantee — not a policy.
